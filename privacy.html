ShotDay Privacy Policy

Effective 9 July 2026

The short version

ShotDay has no account system and no server. Everything you log — doses, injection sites, weight, check-ins, notes — is stored only on your iPhone, protected by your device's encryption and included in your own iCloud/device backups under your control.

What we collect

Nothing. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There are no analytics SDKs, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party tracking in the app.

Apple Health

If you enable Apple Health sync (optional, off by default), ShotDay writes your weight entries to Apple Health on your device. ShotDay never reads data from Apple Health, and Health data never leaves your device through ShotDay.

Purchases

Subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We receive no payment details.

Data export & deletion

You can export your data (CSV/PDF) from Settings at any time. Deleting the app deletes all app data from your device.

Children

ShotDay is intended for adults using prescribed GLP-1 medication.

Changes & contact
